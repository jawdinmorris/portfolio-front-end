My Mission

I first began my development journey in 2017. Immediately I felt a passion for the empowerment education provided. I am currently improving my full-stack web developer skillset. Currently my skills include HTML5, CSS3, JS and Ruby. Software development provided a new gateway; accessibility to myself and others. Access to community, information, education and other vital services. I am working to create a more accessible world through web-based software development.

Web Development

I am currently confident creating front-end web development projects. I am currently expanding upon these skills at Coder Academy Brisbane. I have already established solid skills in Ruby, HTML and CSS. In the coming weeks I will expand on my knowledge on frameworks such as BootStrap and React. On the back-end i'm currently learning the MERN stack.

Accessibility & Community

Accessibility is my driving point for being a developer and the purpose within my work. No human should have difficulty accessing our physical or digital world. Communities drive change and traditional disabilities should not exclude you from creating. In all my work I aim to create a discussion on accessibility and inclusiveness.

Machine Learning

I currently have an interest in expanding my knowledge of machine learning and big data. Personally, data visualisation and journalism have massively influenced my life. The ethics of algorithms rely on the teams developing them. I wish to be part of an inclusive future powered by ML. I'm actively searching for a mentor for this topic, if you can assist please contact me below.
