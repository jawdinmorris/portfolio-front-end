Corporate

I am able to work efficiently and effectively with businesses in order to create full-stack and front-end websites and craft a dynamic and engaging online presence. Individual

I aim to foster a culture that creates change on an individual level, through the use of free educational resources based on accessibility-themed technology. By impacting people personally to begin with, I hope to facilitate communities to teach those who are disadvantaged and less abled proper software development and technology.

Community

Participating, orchestrating and executing local community workshops that focus on teaching children to code as well as reaching out to disadvantaged youths through technology is another area I am extremely passionate about. Through these outreach programs, I hope to be able to solve community issues by working with locals and enabling them with the tools they need to ensure great physical access to their local area.
