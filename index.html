My Mission

I first began my development journey in 2017. Immediately I felt a passion for the empowerment education provided. I am currently seeking new opportunities that will allow me to conitnue improving my full-stack web developer skillset. Software development provided a new gateway; accessibility to myself and others. Access to community, information, education and other vital services. I am working to create a more accessible world through web-based software development.

Technical Skills

I am currently confident creating full-stack web development projects. Currently my learned languages include C#, .Net, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, TypeScript, Ruby and Rails. I have experience in startups, education and consulting using agile methodology. I pride myself on consistently learning and experimenting which has led to personal experience in Unity, game development, python and cyber-security.

Accessibility & Community

Accessibility is my driving point for being a developer and the purpose within my work. No human should have difficulty accessing our physical or digital world. Communities drive change and traditional disabilities should not exclude you from creating. In all my work I aim to create a discussion on accessibility and inclusiveness.

I aim to be an integral part of the local tech community, especially those traditionally underrepresented. I am proud to be a co-organiser of BrisTechNewbies and a regular speaker at local meetups.

Cyber Security

I currently have an interest in expanding my knowledge of cyber security and big data. Personally, sensitive data and the repercussions of an insecure data-world have massively influenced my life as a disabled person. The ethics of data and architecture rely on the teams developing them. I wish to be part of an inclusive future powered by protected data. I'm actively searching for a mentor for this topic, if you can assist please contact me below.
